About me:

I'm a Junior Game Designer who has been able to develop a variety of games through my studies and personal projects, working with diferent engines and technologies.

While my main focus has been Game Design, I've taken different roles, which gave me a wide variety of skills and understanding of how all disciplines correlate and their production pipelines.

I love learning new things and pouring that knowledge into making games, as I hope to keep doing it for the years to come.

Aside from game development, I'm into drawing (check my Instagram if you want!), baked goods, tabletop rpgs (have I already mentioned I'm creating my own system?), board games and not surprisingly, video games!
